# Crypto Algorithm Formula & Group Theory Master Table

| 算法 / Algorithm | 核心公式 / Core Formula | 群结构 / Group Structure | 群阶 / Order | 困难问题 / Hard Problem | 时间复杂度 / Time Complexity | 安全复杂度 / Security Complexity |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **RSA** | $c = m^e \bmod n$ <br> $m = c^d \bmod n$ <br> $n = pq$ <br> $ed \equiv 1 \pmod{\varphi(n)}$ | $(\mathbb{Z}/n\mathbb{Z})^\times$ <br> 有限交换乘法群 <br> Finite Abelian Multiplicative Group | $\varphi(n) = (p-1)(q-1)$ <br> **(未知 / unknown)** | 大整数分解 <br> Integer Factorization <br> (RSA Assumption) | $O((\log n)^3)$ <br> 模幂运算 <br> modular exponentiation | **亚指数 / Sub-exp** <br> $L_n[1/3, c]$ <br> (GNFS) |

---

> **时间复杂度** / **Time Complexity**:
> $O((\log N)^3)$ is polynomial time (slower with longer keys); $O(1)$ is constant time (independent of key length).

> **安全复杂度** / **Security Complexity**:
> "Sub-exponential" means attack difficulty is between polynomial and exponential; "Exponential" is the ideal security level.
